Parameters are separated by spaces, to input a parameter with spaces, wrap it in quotation marks: "<parameter>"

**Embeds**
?embed_simple <title> <body> <color> <image url>
?embed_json <json>
?embed_channel <channel>

**Suggestions**
?suggest <suggestion>
?suggestion_channel <channel>
?suggestion_color <color>

**Definitions**
?define <word>
